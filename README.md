This challenge details the most popular gaming amongst different age groups by deriving the total counts, percentage of gender categorization, max played game, most profitable game according to the age group.

# Analysis Summary
1. 50% of the player gender demographics are Male in the Age group of 30s-40s. 33% of players in the 20s age group. least number of players in the age group of 50s. 

2. Maximum number of purchase count (291) is also in this group of age range 30s-40s where the Total Purchase Price is the maximum ($871.05).

3. During the gender based grouping our analysis reveals that the Non disclosed gender group spent the highest amount per item as compared to the Male and Female group. The item purchase count is not significant as compared to the Male and Female groups. 
        Count	Purchase Average	Purchase Total	Average Purchase Per Person
Gender			
Female	81	    $3.17	            $256.43	      $0.12
Male	  484	    $3.05	            $1,474.70	    $0.02
Other 	11	    $3.41	            $37.52	      $0.87

4. The most popular and profitable purchase has been the Item Feral Katana with a price of $4.40 and purchase count of 4, giving it a significant purchase total of $17 and the other item being Abyssal Shard with good purchase count of 5 thus giving it a good purchase total of $13.35.
